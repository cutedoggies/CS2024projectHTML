About Us

SG Money Lender was established with the aim of providing the best credit service to the public. Our founder was a single mother who borrowed from credit companies to pay for her children's education, but eventually found herself in an endless cycle of debt. Wanting to help fellow Singaporeans in similar situations, she founded SG Money Lender. As a professional and licensed moneylending company, we take pride in being an alternative for loans from the banks and are committed to providing the highest level of customer satisfaction.
