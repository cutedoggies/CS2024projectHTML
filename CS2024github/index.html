About Us

SG Money Lender was established with the aim of providing THE best credit service to the public. Our founder, Lily Lee, was a single mother and struggling to pay for her children's education. She took 3 loans from established companies in this market, but within 4 years, found herself in an endless cycle of trying to pay off all her loans and interests. She decided to do something about this to help fellow Singaporeans break out of this cycle. SG Money Lender is a professional and licensed moneylending company, and our mission is to give not just temporary happiness, but forever freedom. We pride ourselves as an alternative for loans from the banks and customer satisfaction is our main priority.
