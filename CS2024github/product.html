What is a personal loan?

A personal loan is also known as an unsecured loan. These loans are usually used by borrowers to finance big purchases or unexpected expenses. With a personal loan, you can take care of such expenses within a short time period.



When should you apply for a personal loan?

It is always important to carefully consider your financial situation before taking out a loan. However, a personal loan is sometimes the best way to finance a large purchase that you cannot afford upfront. Here are the top six reasons to get a personal loan.

  1. Debt consolidation
  2. Alternative to a payday loan
  3. Home remodeling
  4. Moving costs
  5. Emergency expenses
  6. Large purchases


How do you apply for a personal loan with SG Money Lender?

We make this process as simple and convenient as possible. After clicking on the "Apply Now" button at the bottom of this page, simply fill in the fields that are required.
Do take note of the following as well:

How do I repay my loan?

There are two ways you can repay your loan:

  1. Pay cash at our office
  2. Bank transfer


What is so different about SG Money Lender?

We allow you to pay back your loans using a flexible repayment plan. Our loan solutions are catered to your needs, and our structured repayment plan is straightforward and easy to understand.



What should you look out for before taking a loan?

Some tips:
  • Read and understand the loan statement thoroughly. Ask when in doubt.
  • Do not rush into a decision. Think, analyse, then decide.
  • Understand all the terms and conditions clearly. Feel free to ask our Customer Happiness Experts should you need further clarification.
