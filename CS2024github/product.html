What is a personal loan?


A personal loan also called as unsecured loan. These loans are often used by borrowers for small purchases or unexpected expenses that assist borrowers with their finance. With a personal loan, you can meet your financial requirements taking care of all kinds of expenses in the short time period.


When should I apply for a loan?


It is always important to carefully consider your financial situation before taking on a loan. That being said, sometimes a personal loan is the best way to finance a large purchase project that you cannot afford upfront. Here are the top six reasons to get a personal loan.
  1. Debt consolidation
  2. Alternative to a payday loan
  3. Home remodeling
  4. Moving costs
  5. Emergency expenses
  6. Large purchases


How do I apply for a personal loan with SG Money Lender?


We make this process as simple, easy and pain-free as possible. After clicking on the "Apply Now" button below, simply fill in the fields that are required, including the following:
  1. You need to apply with your NRIC and latest pay slips (if available).
  2. You need to be employed.
  3. You are required to retrieve your last 12 months CPF contribution history statement.

Our Customer Happiness Experts will then get in touch with you within 2 days to better understand your needs and assist you as best we as can.

Some tips:
